Kate Oh- Lenticular Lotus Red, 2019

Kate Oh is a Korean contemporary artist based in the States. She creates artworks inspired by tradtional Korean art called Minwha. This is one out of many from her Lotus painting series. The usages of colors and subject piece shows the beauty of Korean culture through symbolism.
